## StoryBrand BrandScript

### Element 1 — The Character (The Hero)

**The hero is always the parent** — CE-MMA is the guide who gives them the tools to succeed.

**Who they are:**
A parent who loves their child deeply and wants to give them every advantage in life. They're not looking for a karate class — they're looking for a program that instills the confidence and discipline their child needs to truly thrive: in school, in relationships, and in the challenges life will inevitably bring.

**What they want:**
A child who grows up confident in who they are, disciplined in how they work, and resilient enough to handle anything. A child who doesn't just survive the hard moments — they rise to them.

---

### Element 2 — The Problem (Three Layers)

**External Problem** *(what they see happening)*
Their child struggles with confidence, gives up when things get hard, gets overwhelmed by conflict, or can't seem to find focus and motivation — no matter what the parent tries.

**Internal Problem** *(what they feel inside)*
"I want so much for my child — but I don't know how to give them the confidence and discipline they need to actually thrive. I'm worried they're going to struggle through life without it."

**Philosophical Problem** *(what's at stake)*
Every child deserves the chance to grow into a confident, disciplined, capable person. Confidence and discipline aren't personality traits you're born with — they're skills that have to be built. And the window to build them well is now.

---

### Element 3 — The Guide (Competitive Edge MMA)

**Empathy:**
We know how much you want for your child — and how frustrating it is to watch them struggle when you don't know how to reach them. Every parent who walks through our doors wants the same thing: a child who grows up truly ready for life.

**Authority:**
We've spent over 20 years developing a character-first methodology called Valueism — a systematic approach to building confidence and discipline through real, incremental challenges. Our instructors are trained to earn each child's trust first, then push them in exactly the right ways. We've helped 800+ families across two locations watch their children transform — not just on the mat, but in the classroom, at home, and in life.

---

### Element 4 — The Plan

**Step 1 — Book a free introductory class**
No commitment required. Bring your child in and watch our character-first methodology in action. Takes 60 seconds to reserve their spot.

**Step 2 — We build trust, then build confidence and discipline**
Our instructors first earn your child's trust, then introduce the right challenges at the right time — creating real wins that build real confidence from the inside out.

**Step 3 — Watch them thrive — in the gym and beyond**
The confidence, discipline, and resilience they build here follows them everywhere — school, friendships, challenges, and every hard thing life brings their way.

---

### Element 5 — Call to Action

**Direct CTA:**
*"Book Your Child's Free Class"* — used on every hero, section bottom, and page. Clear, specific, benefit-adjacent.

**Transitional CTA:**
*"Download: 5 Signs Your Child Is Ready to Build Real Confidence"* — captures undecided parents who need more before committing.

---

### Element 6 — Failure (Cost of Inaction)

- A child who grows up unsure of themselves — struggling to handle pressure, conflict, or setbacks
- Confidence and discipline that never get built during the window when they're easiest to develop
- A teenager and young adult who can't push through hard things because they never had to
- A parent who wishes they'd given their child this foundation sooner

---

### Element 7 — Success (The After Picture)

- Your child walks into every room with confidence — because they know who they are and what they're capable of
- They push through hard things instead of quitting — because they've done it before and they know they can
- Their focus improves, their grades follow, and their teachers notice a different kid
- They handle conflict — with bullies, with peers, with life — from a place of strength, not fear
- You gave them something that goes far beyond a hobby. You gave them a foundation for life.
